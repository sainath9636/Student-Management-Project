MALLA REDDY COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

Malla Reddy College of Engineering & Technology (MRCET) is a constituent College of Malla Reddy Group of Institutions (MRGI) established in 2004.

The College offers Engineering courses (B.Tech.) in AE, CSE, CSE(AI&ML), CSE(CS), CSE(IOT), CSE(DS), AI&ML, AI&DS, ECE, EEE, IT, MECH disciplines and Post Graduate (M.Tech.) courses in ECE, CSE, MECH and AE specializations in addition to MBA programmes. The College campus with its aboriculture and horticulture is situated away from the hustle and bustle of the city provides a serene and tranquil atmosphere, which is conducive to both teaching and learning processes.
